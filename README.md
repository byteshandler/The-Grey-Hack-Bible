# The Grey Hack Bible

> A comprehensive knowledge base featuring structured lectures, in-depth explanations, Greyscript documentation, multiplayer strategies, practical projects, and technical reference material built for players who want to understand the game, not just play it.

---
## What is this?

The Grey Hack Bible is an attempt to document Grey Hack properly.

Most information about the game is scattered across Discord messages, forgotten forum threads, outdated videos, incomplete wikis, and guides that explain _what_ to do without ever explaining _why_.

This repository exists to change that.

Whether you're launching your first terminal, writing your first Greyscript program, building infrastructure, surviving multiplayer, or refining advanced operational workflows, this Bible is designed to help you understand the systems behind the game instead of blindly following instructions.

If your goal is to copy commands until something works, there are plenty of guides for that.

If your goal is to understand _why_ it works, you're in the right place.

---
## Philosophy

This project is built around a simple idea:

> Understanding beats memorization.

The goal is not to teach you which button to press.

The goal is to teach you why you're pressing it.

Most players learn commands.

The best players learn systems.

By understanding the systems behind the game, you'll be able to adapt, troubleshoot, automate, and solve problems independently instead of relying on walkthroughs, shortcuts, and cargo-cult solutions.

The objective isn't to create another shitty guide.

The objective is to create the resource that should have existed in the first place.
