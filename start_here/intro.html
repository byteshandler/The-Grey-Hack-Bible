What is Grey Hack?

Grey Hack is a massively multiplayer hacking simulator game. You're a hacker with full freedom to act as you wish on a vast network of procedurally generated computers.
Steam Page

The Hacker's Mindset

Whether you're launching your first terminal, writing your first Greyscript program, building infrastructure, surviving multiplayer, or refining advanced operational workflows,
the goal remains the same:
Understand the code.

Most players learn commands, (the "how-to")
The best players learn systems! (the "why")


    Live, Laugh, LUB your code:

  • (Learn)
    Take your own notes.
    Make your it own, whatever makes it easier to remember!
    Dont just copy and paste. Show you're understanding.


  • (Understand)
    Break examples on purpose and FIX them.
    Figure out HOW and WHY its functioning.


  • (Build)
    Test what you knowledge, build small tools from what you learn.

Reminder:
When the code breaks, we bash our heads. NOT the keyboard!

You WILL get caught if you are reckless. It's up to you to learn from the experience!
